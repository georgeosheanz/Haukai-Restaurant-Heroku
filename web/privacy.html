Privacy

The Privacy Act 2020 requires New Zealand organisations to be transparent about how, when, and why they collect personal information. This page will inform you of how The Haukai Restaurant collects and uses personal information.

Information

We collect personal information from you, including your: name, contact information and how people interact with us (for the analytics).

Collection

We collect your personal information by asking directly through the Google booking form or by using website cookies.

Purpose

The information is collected for the purpose of making reservation bookings at The Haukai Restaurant and to gather analytical information about how the website is used to allow us to improve the user experience.

Sharing

We don’t share any information with third-parties about individuals.

Optional Info

People have a choice in whether to provide some types of personal information. If name and contact information are not provided, we won’t be able to make a reservation booking for you at the Haukai Restaurant.

Contact

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at: info@haukai‑restaurant.co.nz.
