The Haukai Restaurant

The Haukai Restaurant began in 2020 devoted to the exploration and development of Maori cooking techniques and ingredients. Māori were great innovators of kai (food) and when (land). Developing their own style of earth cookery (hāngi) and successfully cultivating plants and vegetables brought with them from Hawaiki (ancient homeland) to the much colder environment of Aotearoa.

Over several centuries, these methods have been passed down, refined and still feed the tāngata (people) of Aotearoa today. Situated in beautiful Kerikeri, located in the Bay of Islands, The Haukai Restaurant does their best to celebrate the best of what’s local and in season, making sure to keep their dining experience fresh, exciting and surprising!
